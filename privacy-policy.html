1. What We Collect

Anonymous Usage Analytics

We use PostHog to collect anonymous analytics about how the extension is used. This includes:

  • Page URLs where the extension runs (e.g., rightmove.co.uk/properties/123)
  • Event types (e.g., "prediction completed", "OCR started")
  • Property metadata (bedrooms, postcode district, price ranges)
  • Extension version and site name

Local Storage

We store one randomly-generated identifier locally on your device to distinguish unique installations for analytics aggregation. This ID is not linked to your identity and is deleted when you uninstall the extension.

Property Data (Processed Only)

The extension reads property details from rental listing pages you visit. This data is processed entirely in your browser to generate rent estimates and is never sent to our servers.

4. Data Retention

  • Analytics events: 90 days
  • Local anonymous ID: Until extension uninstalled
  • Property data: Not retained (processed transiently)

5. Your Rights

Under GDPR and UK GDPR, you have the right to:

  • Access: Request a copy of data associated with your anonymous ID
  • Deletion: Uninstall the extension or request analytics deletion
  • Opt-out: Block analytics by blocking us.i.posthog.com
